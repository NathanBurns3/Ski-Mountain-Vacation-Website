Okemo Mountain

Description

Okemo Mountain Resort is home to Vermont's highest vertical drop. The ski resort is located in the town of Ludlow, Vermont, United States and spans 5 distinct alpine areas. Okemo Mountain Resort is a family-oriented and family-operated resort that is designed for skiers of all types and levels. Parents Magazine rated it the Top US Family Snow Resort.

Highlights

  • Highest vertical drop in Vermont (2,200 feet)
  • Rated the top US family snow resort by Parents Magazine
  • Experienced 600,000 skier visits in 2009

Opening and Closing (21/22 Ski Season)

  • Opening Date: November 20, 2021
  • Closing Date: April 17, 2022

Killington Resort

Description

Killington Mountain Resort & Ski Area has the larget vertical drop in New England. The resort is located in Rutland Country, Vermont, United States, near the town of Killington. It is the largest ski area in the eastern U.S. and was given the title "Beast of the East."

Highlights

  • Largest vertical drop in New England (3,050 feet)
  • Largest Ski area in the eastern U.S.
  • Starting in the 2013-14 ski season, it was given the title "Beast of the East"

Sugarloaf

Description

Located on Sugarleaf Mountain in Carrabassett Valley, western Maine. Sugarloaf is the second largest ski resort east of the Mississippi in terms of skiable area and snowmaking percentage. It also has the second longest continuous vertical drop in the New England area of 2,820 feet.

Highlights

  • Only lift-serviced above treeline skiing on the East coast
  • Maine's second highest peak
  • recorded a total of 352,000 skier visits in the 2005-06 season.

Opening and Closing (21/22 Ski Season)

  • Opening Date: November 20, 2021
  • Closing Date: May 2, 2022

Whiteface

Description

Home of the 1980 Winter Olympics, Whiteface Mountain Resort is located in New York and is one of the Highest peaks of the Adirondack Mountains. There are many winter activities available such as bobsledding, snowshoeing, ice skating, luge rides, and visiting the Lake Placid Olympic Museum. During the Summer you can go mountain biking, hiking and 4x4 tours.

Highlights

  • Tons of Winter of Summer activities
  • Hosted the Winter Olympics
  • One of the highest peaks of the Adirondack Mountain
